I am an archivist, digital librarian, poet, and student.

I am currently an MLIS candidate for the Information Studies Department at the University of California, Los Angeles. This past year, I have worked as an Assistant Community Archivist with Visual Communications in Little Tokyo, as an Andrew W. Mellon Foundation Fellow for the Community Archives Lab.

I have experience working at community archives, university archives, digital libraries, natural history museums, and herbaria.

If you would like to get in touch, email me at karlsson.dylan [at] gmail.com.
